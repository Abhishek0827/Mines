How to play

  • Enter the amount you want to bet.
  • Select the maximum number of mines you want.
  • Winning will be the calculated by (bet amount + % of bet amount calculated for the number of mines).
  • Example:
    Bet amount = 200
    Maximum Number of mine = 20
    Winning = 200 + (200/100 * 20)
  • With each diamond block, winning will increase by the above calculation.
  • The game will end if you press any mined block.
